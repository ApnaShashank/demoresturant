A Local Story of Taste and Tradition

Located near S.N. Sinha College in Warisaliganj, Bihari Family Restaurant started with a simple vision: to bring the authentic, rich flavours of Bihar to a modern, comfortable family setting.

We pride ourselves on our fresh cooking, exceptional customer satisfaction, and a dining experience that feels like home. Whether it's a casual dinner or a festive gathering, we make every meal special.
